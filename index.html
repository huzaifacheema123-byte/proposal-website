I made this little story for you because one simple message was not enough.

Every page has something from my heart.

Please read it slowly. I want you to feel every word.

Chapter 1: Ten Years

⏳❤️

Ten years is not a small time.

In these years, life changed, situations changed, priorities changed, and we both changed in many ways.

But one thing did not change for me: my feelings for you.

Chapter 2: Where I Went Wrong

💔

I know I hurt you.

In the beginning, I gave you time, attention, care, and love. But slowly, I started taking you for granted.

I thought you would never go anywhere, so I stopped putting in the same effort. That was my mistake.

I was busy with friends. I was busy with my own things. I used goals and responsibilities as excuses. But the truth is, when you needed me, I was not always there.

Chapter 3: Your Trust

🤍

Now I understand why you have trust issues.

You trusted the version of me who gave love, care, and time. Then later, I became careless with those same things.

I cannot blame you for feeling hurt. Your pain is real, and I respect it.

I am not here to defend myself. I am here to accept what I did wrong.

Chapter 4: My Apology

🙏❤️

I am truly sorry for making you feel alone.

I am sorry for not giving you the time you deserved.

I am sorry for making excuses when all you needed was care, love, and presence.

I cannot change the past, but I can change how I stand with you from now on.

Chapter 5: No More Excuses

🌹✨

I do not want to delay anymore.

I do not want our relationship to stay only on calls, texts, waiting, and uncertainty.

I want to take a real step forward with respect, honesty, and commitment.

❤️ I will give you time, not excuses.
🌹 I will listen when you are hurt.
🤍 I will not take you for granted again.
💬 I will communicate instead of disappearing.
💍 I will take serious steps for our future.
🌸 I will prove love through actions, not only words.

Chapter 6: The Step I Want To Take

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 💍

I want to tell my family about you.

I want us to talk to our parents.

I want to move our relationship toward marriage, not with pressure, but with respect and sincere intention.

Many of my dreams and goals feel connected to you because I imagined my future with you in it.

My love, I know I cannot rebuild trust in one day. I know words alone are not enough. But I want to start again with better actions, better care, and better commitment.

I do not want to lose you because of my carelessness. I want to become the person who makes you feel safe, loved, respected, and chosen.

💍

The Final Question

I know your answer is your choice, and I will respect your heart.

But today, with honesty and love, I want to ask you:

Will you allow me to tell my family about you?

Will you let us talk to our parents and move toward marriage?

Will you marry me? ❤️
